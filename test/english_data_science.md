# Data Science Fundamentals

Data science focuses on extracting reliable insights from data. It combines statistics, programming, and domain knowledge to answer questions and guide decisions.

## Core Concepts

- Data collection and cleaning
- Exploratory analysis and visualization
- Modeling and evaluation
- Communication of results

## Typical Workflow

1. Define the problem and success criteria
2. Gather and validate data sources
3. Prepare features and baselines
4. Train and compare models
5. Review results and share recommendations

## Practical Uses

- Demand forecasting
- Fraud detection
- Customer segmentation
- Recommendation systems

A strong data science practice emphasizes reproducibility, clear metrics, and continuous iteration.
